WHO ARE WE?



At Anukotech, our foundation is built on the principle of "Anuko," which means “think” in Telugu. We are a forward-thinking technology firm committed to quality, innovation, and excellence in the medical field. Our experienced team leverages cutting-edge technologies such as Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and data analytics to empower healthcare organizations and professionals to visualize their success.As a trusted North American partner to industry leaders like ADMAA, IKCON Pharma, and DataNimbus, Anukotech stands at the forefront of technological advancements in healthcare. Our team comprises subject matter experts with vast domain knowledge in medical animation, recruitment, and data science. We are dedicated to addressing the evolving needs of the healthcare sector by providing tailored solutions that integrate advanced technologies and expert insights, fostering growth and innovative potential.
